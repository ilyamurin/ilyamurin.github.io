Pet Boarding and Training

(Spots are limited – Call to find out more)

You can choose between either a 3 or 4 week training schedule.

What to expect? - Your dog will come back transformed.
The same dog but much more aware and cognoscente of its surroundings.
The communication will be much more clear making it easier for you to enjoy your dog.

How it works:

The weeks are broken down to:

  1. Week 1: Getting to know each other and Knowledge week.
  2. Week 2 : Advanced knowledge and applied demands
  3. Week 3: Finalization Week

Week 1 – In order for a dog to get the best from the program they need to trust.
The first week is dedicated to “buying” the dogs trust.
We will go on hikes, walks, drives, etc. to get to know each other.
Slowly as the week continues small implementations are made.

Week 2 - The first week will set the stage for us to proceed to teach your dog more advanced behaviors and skill sets.
The demands are raised everyday to start to pull the best from your friend.

Week 3 – The Final week is dedicated towards fine tuning of all of the knowledge we have gained.
Rarely do we implement new skill sets but this does happen. Most of this period is for sharpening of everything we learned.
