Obedience (Dog Handler 101)

The Art of bringing both human and dog together.
We will teach structure to the dog and skill sets to the owner.

Each lesson works on a new skill set designed to build your confidence in your abilities and your dogs ability to understand you.

On average in 5 lessons you will have a dog OFF LEASH. Thats right!!

  1. First Lesson – In House
  2. Second Lesson – Loose Leash Walking
  3. Third Lesson – Sit / Stay
  4. Fourth Lesson – Streets Safety
  5. Fifth Lesson – Off Leash

(All lessons are done with all the dogs biggest distractions around)

In each lesson we will cover:
dog psychology
k9 body language
street / city agility
how to read terrain properly
as well as covering all the questions you have on various dog problems.

In the end,
my goal is that you will feel superior and confident to how you felt when we met just a few short lessons before.

Behavior Modifications –
Dogs have needs, if there needs are being met than we as the dogs owner shouldn’t have any issues.
If you are having trouble or what I classify as “resistance” in your dog.
We need to figure out how better to serve that need.

Each dog is a bit different,
and environment plays a role but generally there is always something we can do to modify the situation.

Puppy 1 + 2 –
We all love puppies!
But we take a step back is love enough or is there something else we can be giving them that is going to enrich their lives forever…
That is Direction! During

Puppy 1
we go over all the basics of dogs.
From sicknesses, feeding, bathroom needs, gnawing, biting, scratching, stealing from tables,
running out of doors, eating shoes, uncontrollable barking, jumping, and most importantly “The Meaning of the Word NO”.
Many dogs know what this means but have no respect for the word.
Its vital from a young age to implement this in the dog so that they can have an easier time joining our society.
Its all about leading our new responsibility in a direction that will make them a successful adult dog (because one day they will be)

Puppy 2
is designed to be an off leash or basic outdoor session with the dog.
While outdoors we will go over how to make your dog want to follow you.
The most precious time you can spend with a dog is outside so we’ll continue with the training to expand it outdoors.

Security and Protection K9’s
Dogs for thousands of years have been used to alert and protect their human counterparts.
When training a dog to protect they need to have confidence and knowledge of the subject of Combat.
After completeing the obedeince portion in the Jordan Knight Development Program, dogs are now ready to start more advanced training, in this case Bite work.

Dogs are designed to bite on command.
Unlike many militaries and law enforcement organizations every dog I teach has the ability to “Out” (let go) on command.
